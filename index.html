Our Heritage

Born in South India.
Brewed with Precision.
Served with Consistency.

At Madras Coffee House, tradition meets emotion in every sip. Born in 2010 from the vision of founders who wanted to celebrate authentic South Indian filter coffee, our mission was simple — preserve heritage while building something scalable.

From a single outlet in Chennai to a thriving network of 180+ stores, we have grown into a recognized national brand. Every cup follows the same meticulous brewing ritual.
